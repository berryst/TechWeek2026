# Activity 2: 📖 Tell My Aiga's Story

[← Back to Activities](../README.md)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Time** | 5 min |
| **Audience** | Years 4–8 |
| **Skill** | Creative writing with AI as co-author |
| **Tool** | Copilot (text) |

> **Why it works:** Students see AI as a writing partner, not a magic answer machine — their facts shape the story.

## Step-by-step lab

1. Write down 3 true facts about your family on paper or a sticky note.
2. Copy the prompt template and replace the blanks with your own name and family facts.
3. Ask Copilot to write your story, then read it carefully.
4. Add a plot twist, such as a talking treasure, a friendly dragon, or a moment where the hero shares the treasure, and ask Copilot to rewrite the story.
## Prompt template

```text
Write a 6-sentence adventure story for kids about a hero named [STUDENT NAME].The hero's family does these things: [3 FACTS].In the story, [STUDENT NAME] discovers a hidden treasure that helps their family.Use simple words a 9-year-old can read. Make it exciting and end with a happy twist.
```

**Sample prompt 1**

```text
Write a 6-sentence adventure story for kids about a hero named Mila. The hero’s family cooks big Sunday lunches, loves dancing, and visits Nan every weekend. In the story, Mila discovers a hidden treasure that helps their family. Use simple words a 9-year-old can read. Make it exciting and end with a happy twist.
```

**Sample prompt 2**

```text
Write a 6-sentence adventure story for kids about a hero named Sione. The hero’s family plays rugby, grows taro in the garden, and sings together at church. In the story, Sione discovers a hidden treasure that helps their family. Use simple words a 9-year-old can read. Make it exciting and end with a happy twist.
```
