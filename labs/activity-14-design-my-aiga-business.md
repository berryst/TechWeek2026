# Activity 14: 🏪 Design My Aiga Business

[← Back to Activities](../README.md)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Time** | 10 min |
| **Audience** | Years 5–8 |
| **Skill** | Image gen + brand voice + multi-modal Copilot use |
| **Tool** | Copilot (image + text) |

> **Why it works:** Combines image generation, copywriting, and identity-building — and gives kids the language of entrepreneurship in a way that feels playful, not corporate.

## Step-by-step lab

1. Think of a small business your aiga could run and choose what it would sell.
2. Use Prompt 1 to generate 4 logo ideas in Copilot.
3. Choose your favourite logo and improve it with one more detail.
4. Use Prompt 2 to create a name, tagline, and short brand description.
5. Use Prompt 3 to make a colourful poster for your business.
6. Share your business name and tagline with the group.
## Prompt template

```text
PROMPT 1 (logo image):Design a friendly logo for a small Pacific-owned business called [BUSINESS NAME].It sells [PRODUCT].Use [2 COLOURS]. Include a simple icon of [SYMBOL — e.g. a frangipani flower].Flat 2D, clean lines, suitable for a t-shirt or sticker.PROMPT 2 (brand voice):Write a brand identity for a small business called [BUSINESS NAME] that sells [PRODUCT].Give me: a punchy tagline (under 8 words), a short 'about us' paragraph (3 sentences),and 3 sample social media post ideas.Tone: warm, proud, Pacific. Use simple English.PROMPT 3 (poster):Create a colourful A4 market poster for [BUSINESS NAME].Include the words [TAGLINE] in big friendly letters.Show [PRODUCT] in a fun cartoon style.Pacific-inspired pattern border. Bright, eye-catching.
```
